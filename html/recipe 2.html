Ingredients

For the filling

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • ½ cup heavy cream (or milk)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp thyme (optional)

For the crust

  • 2 premade pie crusts (or homemade)
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven - set your oven to 190°C.
  2. Prepare the filling:
  • In a pan, melt butter over medium heat. Sauté onions and garlic until soft.
  • Add flour and stir for 1 minute.
  • Gradually pour in chicken broth while stirring to avoid lumps.
  • Add heavy cream, salt, pepper, and thyme. Let it simmer until thickened.
  • Stir in cooked chicken and vegetables. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
